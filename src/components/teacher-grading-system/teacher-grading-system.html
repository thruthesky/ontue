Teacher Grading System

  • Grade 1
    Having only good english teaching skill does not help to earn grade.
    A Good teacher must have
    • Good Environment/Work Place.
      ( No unwanted sounds, No people at the back, etc... )
    • Good Devices.( PC/Laptop, Headset with mic)
    • Good Internet.( Reasonable Connection )
    • Good Profile Picture
    • Good Video Introduction
    • Prepared and Always ready to teach.
    • Finishing the evaluation on time.
    • Following our promotion schedule.
    • Following our guidelines.
    • Following our policy.
    • Always present in class.
    • Always records their class.
  • Grade 2

    No matter how good you teach, but you failed to communicate with he student. It will not work.

    • Good Communication Skills.
    • Good Teaching Skills.

TEACHER GRADING SYSTEM

Grade 1

Having only good english teaching skill does not help to earn grade. A Good teacher must have, Good Environment/Work Place,( No unwanted sounds, No people at the back, etc... ) Good Devices.( PC/Laptop, Headset with mic), Good Internet.( Reasonable Connection ), Good Profile Picture, Good Video Introduction, Prepared and Always ready to teach, Finishing the evaluation on time, Following our promotion schedule, Following our guidelines, Following our policy, Always present in class, and Always records their class

Grade 2

Communication comes first then the teaching comes next. No matter how good you teach, but you failed to communicate with he student. It will not work. A Good teacher must have a Good Environment/Work Place, Capable of winning student's heart, with Good Communication Skills, and have a Good Teaching Skills.

Grade 3

Consistent Reservation. Good Performance, Good Attitude, and Professionalism. A Good teacher who is Popular to student, Most Requested teacher, Giving Importance to student growth, and Always winning the student's heart

Regular Sessions
  • We strongly request all teacher to follow this format.
  • It is considered a level test class when the session has 0 point.
  • First 2 minutes: Say hello to the student. Ask name, age, location, school.
  • After this, you will know how the student speaks.
  • Then, tell the student that you are going to conduct a simple English level test.
  • 1. Say hello to Student for 2 minutes.
  • 2.1. (Book)Overview the last session or discuss about the book.
    • Continue the book for 15 minutes.
  • 2.2. (Freetalking)Overview the last session or talk about new topic.
    • Freetalking for 15 minutes.
  • 4. Correct the student mistakes like words, expression, etc for 1 or 2 minutes.
  • 5. Review/Recap for 1 or 2 minutes.
  • 6. Discuss about the next class. What book will be used.
    Teacher may ask the student study the book in advance.

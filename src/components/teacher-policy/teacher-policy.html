Bookable Time

Bookable time is an time interval to prevent a sudden booking by student. The input unit is minutes. If you put numbre 1 in bookable time, it means, student cannot book(reserve) your schedule that would begin in 1 minutes. Teachers who wants to be offline often should put 60 or more minutes. But those teacher who put longer bookable time gets less booking because their schedule may not appear on 'immediate booking' menu.
